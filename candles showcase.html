About Me

Hi, I'm Noopur — passionated about the art of candle-making.

Everything started in my small home kitchen — melting soy wax, experimenting with essential oils, trying to capture feelings in a moulds.

Today My Candles are is still 100% hand-poured by me in Lucknow. Every single mould gets love, intention and a little piece of home. No mass production, no shortcuts — just real scents for real moments.

Thank you for being here. I hope one of these candles finds its way into your space and brings you a bit of peace.
